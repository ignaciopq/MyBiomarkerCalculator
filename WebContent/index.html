Welcome to My Biomarkers Calculator

My Biomarkers Calculator is an interactive tool that gives you an estimate of different chemical substances found in your body according to data obtained from NHANES survey.

Learn More Start Calculation

In particular, My Biomarkers Calculator provides information regarding the following toxicants filtered by a number of different factors:

  • Toxicants:
  • Bisphenol A
  • Lead
  • Mercury
  • Cotinine
  • DDE
  • Polychlorinated Biphenyls
  • PDE-47
  • Insesticides (Diethylthiophosphate)
  • Phthalates and Plasticizers
  • Categories:
  • Age
  • Country
  • Gender
  • Income
  • Education
  • Smoking
  • Ethnicity
  • Diet
