My Biomarkers Calculator Explorer

In this section you are allowed to explore NHANES data and obtain chemical levels of different toxicants and filter them according to different categories. For each category, you will see a chart that will allow you to compare results for different people in that category.

To display the chart, you need to click on one category on the left side bar and one toxicant in the upper bar. For example, if you click on "Age" and "Bisphenol A" you will obtain a bubble chart that shows you the median value of this toxicant according to different age groups.
